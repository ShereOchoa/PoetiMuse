Frequently asked questions

What is PoetiMuse?

It is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to generate original poems.

How does PoetiMuse work?

It uses a complex algorithm to analyze language and generate unique, original poems based on user input.

Who owns the poems generated by PoetiMuse?

All poems generated by PoetiMuse are the intellectual property of the user who generated them.

Can I share the poems I generate with others?

Yes, you are free to share the poems you generate with others.

Can I use the poems I generate for commercial purposes?

If you own the intellectual property rights to the poems, you can use them for commercial purposes. However, using the poems for commercial purposes without owning the rights may be illegal.

Can I request a specific type of poem to be generated?

Yes, you can experiment with different input prompts to see what kinds of poems the algorithm generates.

Is there a limit to how many poems I can generate?

There is no limit to how many poems you can generate using PoetiMuse.

Can I edit the poems generated by PoetiMuse?

Yes, you are free to edit the poems.

Are there any restrictions on how I can use PoetiMuse?

Yes, please refer to the Terms of Use for the app for information on prohibited uses and other restrictions.
